How do I connect Apple Health?

Run Stories requests HealthKit access when you first open the app. You can also enable it later in Settings > Health > Run Stories on your iPhone.

How do I connect Strava?

Tap the Strava option on the workout source screen and log in with your Strava credentials. Run Stories will sync your recent activities.

Do I need an account?

No. Run Stories works immediately with Apple Health — no sign-up required. A Strava account is only needed if you want to import Strava activities.

How many free exports do I get?

You get 5 free exports. After that, upgrade to Pro for unlimited exports and access to all premium templates.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

Is my data private?

Yes. All health, workout, and photo data stays on your device. We never collect or send your data to any server. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
