Run Stories

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Run Stories ("we", "the app") is developed by Rapptor. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

Run Stories reads workout data (routes, distance, pace, heart rate, etc.) from Apple HealthKit with your permission. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server. We do not collect, store, or share your health data.

Strava Data

If you connect your Strava account, Run Stories fetches your activity data via the Strava API. This data is cached locally on your device for performance purposes and is never sent to third-party servers. You can disconnect Strava at any time.

Photos

Run Stories accesses your photo library only when you choose a background photo for your Story. Photos are processed on-device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Analytics

We do not collect analytics on your health, fitness, or photo data. The app does not include third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

Subscriptions

Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Storage

All your data — workouts, photos, and created Stories — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data.

Children's Privacy

Run Stories does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at runstories.app/privacy-policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@runstories.app.
